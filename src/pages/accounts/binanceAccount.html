Binance Account

To be able to view your binace account, your open trades and set up auto trades you need to share your API key and your private key with Autotrade.

Go to binance and create an API key and give permission to read account and to trade. Autotrade has no withdraw function, don't give this permission.

For your own safety the keys are never send to this client unmasked.
